Social Media Made Easy!

A step-by-step guide to help you connect with your family and friends

What You’ve probably heard people talk about social media whether they are talking about how social media is changing the world or even talking about some of the dangers of social media. So let’s define social media,'social' simply means interacting with other people by sharing information with them and receiving information from them. The 'media' part is how you communicate with them, like the internet (whereas TV, radio, and newspapers are examples of more traditional forms of media). If you put those terms together, you can come to the conclusion the social media could be any page on the internet that allows it’s users to interact with each other by both sharing and receiving information. Social media tools are dedicated websites used to facilitate social media, examples of these are facebook, twitter, Instagram, Pinterest and so many more. .

Why So why would people choose to sign up to social media? You can easily pick up the phone and call your grandkids or send them a card over Xmas. But what makes social media so interesting. Social media allows you to connect with family and friends whenever you want, and wherever they are. Not everyone has time for a phone call, but if you join a social media tool like facebook, your friends and family will add life updates to be shared with all of their friends. These updates could be messages, pictures or videos. You can add one picture to the website and all of your friends and family will be able to view it and share their thoughts. .

How Every social media tool works differently, but they all allow you to communicate with your friends in a way that suits you. All you have to do is find the social media tool that interests you, sign up and you are good to go. On this website, you will learn how to sign up to some of the most popular social media tools, but you can use what you learn here for anything. Whether it’s to sign up to weekly it’s newsletters, online stores or even a dating website. The possibilities are endless so let’s get started. .
