Social Media Made Easy!

A step-by-step guide to help you connect with your family and friends

What is Social Media?

Social media could be any page on the internet that allows it’s users to interact with each other by both sharing and receiving information. This information can be in the form of pictures, videos or even written messages.

Why is Social Media Useful?

Social media allows you to connect with family and friends whenever you want, and wherever they are. Not everyone has time for a phone call, but if you join a social media tool like facebook, your friends and family will add life updates to be shared with all of their friends.

What Are Social Media Tools?

Social media tools are dedicated websites or mobile apps used to facilitate social media. Some of great examples of these are Facebook, Twitter, Instagram, Pinterest and so many more.
